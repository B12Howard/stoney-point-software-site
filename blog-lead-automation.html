Stop Losing Leads: How to Automate Your Lead Capture

The Problem: Leads Fall Through the Cracks

You're running Facebook ads. You're on Angi. You have a contact form on your website. Leads are coming in but they're scattered across different platforms.

Someone has to manually check each source, copy data into your CRM, and notify the sales team. By the time you get around to it, the lead has already gone with a competitor who responded faster.

This is a common problem for contractors and home service businesses and it's costing you thousands in lost revenue.

The Solution: Automated Lead Capture

When a lead comes in from any source, it should automatically flow into your CRM and notify your team in real-time. No manual copying. No missed opportunities.

Here's what a good lead automation system looks like:

  1. Lead submits form (Google Forms, Facebook Lead Ads, Angi, etc.)
  2. Data flows into CRM (Pipedrive, HubSpot, Salesforce, etc.) automatically
  3. Team gets notified instantly via Slack, SMS, or email
  4. Follow-up begins within minutes, not hours or days

Real-World Example: Contractor Lead Automation

We built this exact system for a contractor who was juggling leads from Google Forms, Angi, and Facebook. They were manually entering data and missing leads in the chaos.

After implementing automated lead capture:

  • Zero missed leads – everything flows into their CRM automatically
  • Instant notifications – sales team gets Slack alerts within seconds
  • Faster response times – they respond to leads while competitors are still checking email
  • Better tracking – they can see exactly where leads come from and what converts
