Case Study 01
📦 Plumbing Company: Automated Quoting System

Before: Staff manually created Google Sheet quotes, copying data from emails and handwritten notes — prone to errors and wasted time.

Solution: We built a dynamic form system that automatically generated templated quotes, version-tracked changes, and routed approvals via email.

⏱️ Saved 10+ hours/week and eliminated 90% of manual errors.
