FF&E Design to Shopping Cart for Faster Customer Approval

A GatherIt integration and internal Chrome extension flow that moves product selections to payment quickly, so projects keep moving and cash comes in faster.

What This Solves

Many remodel teams still rely on email threads and signoff documents to turn FF&E choices into paid orders. That was a problem for a home remodel business in New York. That slows down design phases, adds friction for customers, and delays revenue. A custom Chrome extension that works directly in the GatherIt web app to move selections to payment quickly, so projects keep moving and cash comes in faster.

  • Faster cash collection: Move from selection to payment without signoff documents.
  • Shorter design to purchase cycle: Reduce delays between revisions and approvals.
  • Less admin burden: Replace repetitive handoffs and manual tracking.
  • Use any payment processor: Use your existing credit card payment processor.
  • Payment alerts: Receive notifications when payments are received in Slack or your preferred messaging app.

Ways To Export FF&E Data to a Shopping Cart

1) From the GatherIt Web App

The extension works directly with GatherIt so teams can select project items and create a customer-ready shopping cart. This is the preferred path when teams are actively managing FF&E inside GatherIt.

2) From an Excel File

For teams that still export spreadsheet files, the extension can intercept the downloaded Excel file and to create acustomer-ready shopping cart.

Built in collaboration with GatherIt: We worked directly with the GatherIt FF&E specification platform team early on, tested the API in a live remodel workflow, and shared feedback as we built the integration.

How It Looks

GatherIt + extension workflow: Initial item selection and cart creation.

In-app selection: Products are marked by the design or sales team to create customer-specific shopping carts.

Easy cart creation: Designers and sales move from design decisions to purchasable carts without DocuSign and heavy email back-and-forth.

Customer-facing flow: Cart review, secure payment, and receipt confirmation.

Cart page: Customers review included items and quantities before moving to payment.

Checkout page: Customers pay by credit card in a secure, simple checkout experience.

Receipt and confirmation: The order is finalized with an email receipt and clear totals.

Who This Is For

Best fit for upscale remodel and design-build businesses that run multi-phase design, purchase, redesign, and purchase cycles.

  • Teams using GatherIt for FF&E and needing a cleaner checkout handoff.
  • Teams still using spreadsheets and wanting a lower-friction bridge to payment.
  • Organizations inside Google Workspace that need internal Chrome extension workflows.

Business Outcomes

  • Improved cashflow: Orders are paid sooner via customer credit cards.
  • Faster project momentum: Design and purchasing phases move forward with less waiting.
  • Reduced process friction: Less back-and-forth than DocuSign and email workflows.
  • Higher team confidence: Sales and design staff are less hesitant to ask for payment.
