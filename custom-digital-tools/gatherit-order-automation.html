Turn your FF&E selections into a polished, shoppable experience your clients can browse, approve, and purchase — integrated with your CRM, messaging, and payments.

What This Solves

Interior designers and remodelers spend hours managing the gap between "here's what I've selected" and "here's your invoice." Emails, PDFs, purchase orders, manual follow-up — it slows cash flow and creates friction at exactly the moment a client is ready to commit.

Tote closes that gap. Your FF&E selections become a curated, client-facing experience your customers can browse, approve, and pay through in one flow — without you chasing down approvals or manually entering orders.

Who This Is For

  • Interior designers and design firms managing FF&E selections
  • Remodelers presenting curated product selections to clients
  • Teams currently relying on manual purchase orders and email approvals

How It Works

  • Client browses, approves, and pays in one flow
