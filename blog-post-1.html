How We Migrated 15 Years of Data

When Ironsmith needed to modernize, we faced the challenge of migrating 15 years of business-critical data from a legacy Microsoft Access system to a modern PostgreSQL database. Here's how we did it:

  • Extracted and cleaned hundreds of thousands of records from Access.
  • Rebuilt broken relationships and validated data integrity.
  • Automated the migration pipeline for a seamless transition to AWS-hosted PostgreSQL.

The result? Zero downtime, no data loss, and a future-proof system ready for growth.
