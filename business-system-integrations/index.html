Common Integration Scenarios

Lead Capture to CRM

Automatically send leads from Google Forms, Facebook, or Angi directly into your CRM (Pipedrive, HubSpot, etc.). Get Slack notifications when new leads come in. Never miss an opportunity again.

CRM to Invoicing

When you close a deal in your CRM, automatically create an invoice in QuickBooks. No more manual data entry. Faster billing means faster payment.

Payment Confirmation to Team Notifications

When a customer pays via Stripe or Square, automatically notify your team in Slack and update project status in your CRM or project management tool.

Customer Data Sync

Keep customer information in sync across all your systems. Update once, update everywhere. No more inconsistent records.
