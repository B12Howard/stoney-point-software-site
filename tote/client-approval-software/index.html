The format shapes the response.

Send a PDF and you get a partial reply three days later. "The sofa looks good, not sure about the lamp, what was the third option?" You piece it together, update your records manually, start the payment conversation separately.

Tote is the client-facing portal on top of whatever you already use — you don't have to switch your existing software. Clients see each selection individually and respond to each one. The responses come back structured. Payment happens in the same session, and you're left with one clear record of what was approved.

What Tote does.

  • Presents each selection with images, descriptions, and pricing, in a format built for a decision
  • Collects structured responses: approvals, change requests, and questions, all in one place
  • Connects approval to payment — clients pay in the same flow
  • Confirmed selections integrate with your existing workflow
