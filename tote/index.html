Questions.

What does Tote cost?

$250/month, flat. Not per seat, not per transaction.

Will my price change later?

Not if you're one of our first 10 clients. That $250/month founding rate is locked in for as long as you're a customer, even as pricing changes for anyone who joins after.

Who owns the data?

You do. If you leave, you take it with you.

Whose payment processor handles the transaction?

Yours. Tote connects to the payment processor you already use — client payments go through your account, not ours.

What's involved in setup?

We build it around your business: your CRM, messaging system, branding, privacy policy, payment processor. It's not off-the-shelf software you configure yourself — we integrate it for you before your first client sees a Tote.

Can I edit a Tote after I've sent it?

No — a Tote is fixed once sent. If something needs to change, you send a new one and your client reviews and approves that version.

Does my client need an account?

No. They open the link on any device and go straight to reviewing selections.
