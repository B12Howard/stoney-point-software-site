Real-World Example

Complete Business System: From Legacy to Modern

Ironsmith, a manufacturing company, was stuck on a 15-year-old Microsoft Access system that crashed regularly. Their previous agency abandoned a half-finished modernization project, leaving them with nothing.

We built them a complete business system from scratch:

  • Cloud-based business portal for managing quotes, orders, and operations
  • Integrated WordPress website synced to their product catalog
  • Desktop app to access on-premise file servers from the web portal
  • Migrated 15 years of legacy data from Microsoft Access to PostgreSQL

Result: Ironsmith now has a modern, reliable system that doesn't crash, supports their entire operation, and has 24-hour support. They control their data and can finally scale their business.
